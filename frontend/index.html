Frequently Asked Questions

How does OneTimeView work?

OneTimeView lets you create secure, self-destructing links for messages and files. Simply create your secret, set a view limit (1-10 times), and share the link. Once the recipient views it the specified number of times or the expiry time is reached, the content is permanently deleted from our servers.

Is OneTimeView really free?

Yes! Most features are completely free including text messages, file uploads up to 500MB, password protection, and expiry options (24 hours, 30 days, 6 months). Premium ($9/month) adds the "No Expiry" option, removes ads, and provides priority support.

What types of files can I share?

You can share any file type including images (JPEG, PNG, GIF), videos (MP4, AVI, MOV), documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT), archives (ZIP, RAR), and more. The maximum file size is 500MB for all users.

How secure is my data?

Your data is highly secure. You can add password protection for extra security. All content is automatically deleted after being viewed or when it expires. We don't log, track, or store your sensitive information beyond the lifetime of your secret link.

What happens if I share the link with multiple people?

You can set a view limit from 1-10 views. With a one-time view (default), the secret is deleted after the first person opens it. If you set a higher limit, multiple people can view it until the maximum views are reached or the expiry time passes.

Can I retrieve a secret after it's been deleted?

No. Once a secret reaches its view limit or expires, it is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our servers. This ensures maximum privacy and security. Make sure the recipient has access to the content before it's deleted.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required for free features. You can start sharing secrets immediately without any registration. This ensures your privacy and makes the service quick and easy to use.

What is One Time View?

A one-time view (also called ephemeral messaging or self-destructing messages) is a feature that allows you to send content that automatically disappears after being viewed once. This can include:

  • Photos and videos - Images that delete after viewing
  • Text messages - Notes that vanish after reading
  • Files and documents - Attachments that self-destruct
  • Voice notes - Audio messages with limited playback

This feature is available on many popular messaging platforms like WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, and Snapchat. However, each platform has its own implementation and limitations.

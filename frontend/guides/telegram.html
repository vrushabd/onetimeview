Telegram Self-Destructing Messages

Telegram offers two main types of disappearing content:

  • Self-Destruct Timer - Messages that delete after a set time (in Secret Chats)
  • View Once Media - Photos and videos that delete after being viewed once

How to Send View Once Media on Telegram

  1. 1Open any chat in Telegram
  2. 2Attach a photo or video
  3. 3Tap the timer icon
  4. 4Select "View Once"
  5. 5Send the media

FAQ

Can you screenshot Telegram View Once photos?

Yes, but in Secret Chats, the sender will be notified. In regular chats, there's no screenshot notification.

How long does View Once media last on Telegram?

View Once media stays available until the recipient views it, then immediately deletes. There's no time limit before viewing.

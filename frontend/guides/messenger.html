Messenger Disappearing Content Features

Facebook Messenger offers two main features for disappearing content:

  • Vanish Mode - Messages that disappear after they're seen and you close the chat
  • View Once Photos/Videos - Media that can only be viewed once

How to Send View Once Media on Messenger

  1. 1Open a conversation in Messenger
  2. 2Tap the camera or photo icon
  3. 3Select or capture a photo/video
  4. 4Select "View Once" option
  5. 5Send the media

FAQ

Does Messenger notify screenshots of View Once photos?

Yes, Messenger notifies both users when a screenshot is taken of View Once media.

Can you save View Once photos on Messenger?

No official way exists. Screenshots are detected and the sender is notified.

How is Vanish Mode different from Secret Conversations?

Secret Conversations use end-to-end encryption and are device-specific. Vanish Mode just makes messages disappear but doesn't provide E2E encryption.

What is Instagram One Time View?

Instagram's View Once (one-time view) feature allows you to send disappearing photos and videos in Direct Messages that can only be viewed once by the recipient. After viewing, the media is automatically deleted from the chat.

How to Send One Time View Photos on Instagram

  1. 1Open Instagram Direct Messages
  2. 2Select a conversation or start a new one
  3. 3Tap the camera icon or select a photo
  4. 4Toggle "View Once" option
  5. 5Send the media

Can You Screenshot Instagram One Time View?

Yes, but... Instagram does notify the sender when someone takes a screenshot of a View Once photo or video in DMs. This is different from regular Stories where screenshots aren't always detected.

Privacy Note: If you screenshot a View Once message, the sender will be notified.

Limitations of Instagram View Once

  • Only available in Direct Messages (not regular posts or stories)
  • Limited to Instagram platform - can't share cross-platform
  • No password protection option
  • No custom view count (always exactly 1 view)
  • Screen recording may not be detected on all devices

FAQ: Instagram One Time View

Does Instagram notify for screen recordings?

Screen recording detection is inconsistent. Instagram may not always notify the sender when View Once content is screen recorded.

Can you replay a View Once message?

No, once you open a View Once message on Instagram, it's immediately deleted and cannot be viewed again.

What if I accidentally open a View Once message?

Unfortunately, there's no way to "un-view" it. The message is gone as soon as you tap it.
